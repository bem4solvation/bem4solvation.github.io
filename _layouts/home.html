About

Welcome! This website describes the activities of the research group led by Dr. Christopher D. Cooper, based at the Departament of Mechanical Engineering of Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María (USM), in Valparaíso, Chile. Our research interest is in the development of fast and accurate computational models for molecular electrostatics and solvation, mainly using implicit solvent models and boundary integral methods.
